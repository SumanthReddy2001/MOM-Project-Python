How it works

  • Begin by uploading your meeting video recording to our platform.
  • Our system swiftly converts the video into an audio format.
  • The audio file is securely uploaded to your designated Amazon S3 storage.
  • AWS Transcribe accurately transcribes the audio content into text.
  • Our advanced algorithms process the transcribed text, extracting key points and action items.
  • Finally, organized meeting minutes are generated in PDF format for easy sharing and reference.
